Core elements

There are 3 core elements of our project. These core elements are rooted in spreading awereness of the different cultures that make up the College of Engineering. Hence, down below are the outcomes of our project. Scroll down below to see a full description of each core element!

For this element, the team focused on creating a physical object to represent the diveristy within the college. Moreover, a world map was placed in Duke. Hence, the purpose of the map is for students and UNC Charlotte staff to pinpoint where they are from. All with the idea to make people see how diverse the College of Engineering is and make students from different backgrounds feel included.

An objective of the team was to educate students on how to communicate across culture to bridge that gap. Hence, a speaker series was coordinated alongside Dr. Joseph Hoff from UNC Charlotte Office of Global Education and Engagement. Dr. Hoff gave an interactive workshop highlighting the differences in cultures values and perceptions. As well as, Tayo Rockson who is an author of "Use your Difference to Make A Difference" who gave insights into internal and external cultures, biases, triggers, values, perpetuation systems, and power dynamics. In addition, we distributed 10 copies of Tayo Rockson's book for students to further engage on how to communicate effectively.

Along with the map, the team aimed to create a website which you are currently viewing! Hence, with this website, the team wanted for you to be able to express and connect with other student's experiences via the 'Experiences Forum'. As well as, to be able to learn and use the resources availble at UNCC. Learn more down below!
