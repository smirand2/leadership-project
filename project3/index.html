LEADERSHIP ACADEMY

The Leadership Academy is a two-year, extracurricular program modeled after leadership training programs for young professionals in industry. It prepares undergraduate students to successfully fulfill leadership roles on campus and within the engineering industry by building their leadership abilities. The Leadership Academy requires their participants to complete a community service capstone project at the end to complete the program.

COHORT 20 TEAM

- Cultural Bridge Builders


Three students came together as a team with the purpose of creating an inclusive community within the UNC Charlotte College of Engineering. Hence, the team hopes to bridge a path to a deeper understanding of the diverse cultures and people's experineces within these cultures. From which is at its most critical point, as the workforce and personal relationships are becoming more globalized every day. The team hopes to build the foundation in the present so that students going into the workforce are more aware, knowledgeable, and prepared for global connections.

Sara Miranda

Industrial & Systems Engineering Student

Sara Miranda is a student leader for two organizations on campus which are the Latin American Student Organizations and Latinx of Engineering. These extracurriculars help her build her leadership skills and network. Meanwhile, in the classroom she dives into her passion for learning. Her interest lie in continous improvement, quality engineering, and operations.

Alexandria Traynham

Mechanical Engineering Student

Alexandria Jeanann Traynham is a Mechanical Engineering student as well as a member of the University Honors Program, the Helping Hand Chapter at UNCC, and an engineering mentor for the Maximizing Academic and Professional Success Program. Academically, her interests lie in specializing within the biomedical engineering program - where she researches regeneration of degenerated neurons as a result of chemo-therapy induced peripheral neuropathy.

Molina Williams

Civil Engineering Technology Student

Molina Williams is an engineering student at UNC Charlotte studying Civil Engineering Technology Applied Energy and Sustainable Systems.
